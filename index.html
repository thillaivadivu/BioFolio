Gene Expression in HeLa Cells Under Hypoxia

Investigating upregulation of HIF-1α targets under oxygen-depleted conditions...

qPCRWestern BlotRNA-seq
Project Details

This study analyzed 48 gene targets in HeLa cells cultured at 1% O₂ for 24h, 48h, and 72h. Found 23 significantly upregulated hypoxia-responsive genes, including VEGF, LDHA, and GLUT1. Results contributed to the department's cancer metabolism dataset.

Antibiotic Resistance in E. coli Clinical Isolates

Screening 120 isolates for multi-drug resistance phenotypes and genotypes...

PCRMICPlasmid
Project Details

Conducted MIC assays and identified blaCTX-M resistance genes in 34% of isolates using multiplex PCR. Plasmid conjugation experiments confirmed horizontal gene transfer pathways. Submitted to the AMR national database.

Cataloguing native versus invasive plant species across 14 urban green zones...

eDNAGISField Survey
